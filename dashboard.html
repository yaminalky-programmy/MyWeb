How it works

  • Your query is forwarded through MyWeb's servers — your IP is never sent to the results source.
  • No AI-generated summary is injected into results — you see clean, organic results only.
  • No search query is logged. No session is tied to a profile. No retargeting.
  • Sign-in is never required to search.
